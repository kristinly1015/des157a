Instructions: There are two alien players. The first player is randomized and your goal is to throw meteor(s) at the other player. If you throw a single cold meteor, your turn is skipped. After each throw, the player can either throw again, (assuming a cold meteor wasn't rolled) or pass the turn. The first person who reaches 30 meteor throws wins and takes over the galaxy!

Warning: If you throw two single "cold meteors" (galactic error), your score gets zeroed out.
